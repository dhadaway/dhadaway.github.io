Kea: Learn Birds Through Play

Kea is an app that is helping a technology-immersed society connect with the birds that share our planet. Kea helps us learn birds faster, easier, and with more fun than any rote memorization, by turning the process of learning to recognize birds into a game. To make things even better, Kea already covers more of the globe than any field guide.
